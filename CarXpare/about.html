About CarXpare

One website for all automobile details, the website is designed with the moto of helping the customer to compare different models of cars and also get some recommendations for better results.The website provides easy and creative approach to compare the cars.The website follows up the amazing black and white theme.The key features of the website are as it provides maintenance service, provides renting your ride and also a Ecommerce website for automobile shopping . The company has achieved the certification of A+ by AutoExpo for the great outlook of the website and overwhelming customer positive responses for the website.

About the Project

This website has been designed under the ESD (employability skill development) session organized by Thakur college of engineering and technology. The moto of this ESD is to understand the Html, CSS, Javascript, Json, bootstrap-5 and Xampp for local hosting and implement all this knowledge on our project.A group of 5 students have to perform such task to get hands on experience and also explore team work and project management skills.Our team selected this topic -Website to compare the automobiles ,as this helps us to share the automobile details through our project using coding as a skill.
